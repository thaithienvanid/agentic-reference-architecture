# Multi-pass refinement report

**Date:** July 12, 2026  
**Scope:** normative contracts, security/compliance, operations, examples, research presentation, and automated checks.

## Pass 3 — Operational and implementation quality

Completed:

- Added provider model qualification, drift, deprecation, forced withdrawal, migration, canary, and rollback.
- Added load, soak, chaos, DR, penetration, red-team, sandbox, and model-lifecycle testing.
- Added a worked saga and compensation pattern.

## Remaining assurance obligations

The documentation is not a formal legal opinion, security assessment, conformity assessment, penetration test, or operational certification. Implementations still require domain-specific threat modeling, provider/vendor review, legal/privacy analysis, performance testing, recovery exercises, evaluator calibration, and independent assurance appropriate to their risk.

## Recommendation

The architecture is materially more coherent and implementation-ready after these passes. Future changes should modify the canonical ontology, conventions, gates, ports, diagrams, schemas, examples, and checks together rather than restating core contracts independently.
