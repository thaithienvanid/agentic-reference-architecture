# Comparison matrices

Scores indicate architectural fit from 1 (weak) to 5 (strong), not overall product quality or popularity.

## Clean, Hexagonal, and ARA

| Criterion | Clean | Hexagonal | ARA |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Inward dependency direction | 5 | 4 | 5 |
| Explicit driving/driven ports | 3 | 5 | 5 |
| Domain isolation | 5 | 4 | 5 |
| Durable execution model | 1 | 1 | 5 |
| Nondeterministic effect boundary | 2 | 2 | 5 |
| Human suspension/resumption | 1 | 1 | 5 |
| Idempotency/reconciliation | 1 | 2 | 5 |
| Budgets/runaway control | 1 | 1 | 5 |
| Replay/evaluation/platform planes | 1 | 1 | 5 |

ARA extends rather than replaces Clean and Hexagonal principles.

## Agent frameworks versus durable engines

| Criterion | Agent framework | Durable workflow engine |
|---|---:|---:|
| Model/tool-loop authoring | 5 | 1 |
| Context/prompt ergonomics | 5 | 1 |
| Durable waits/timers/signals | 3 | 5 |
| Deterministic recovery | 2–3 | 5 |
| Streaming | 5 | 2 |
| Enterprise worker operations | 3 | 5 |
| Recommended role | Bounded agent logic | Lifecycle durability |

Recommended combination: durable engine for lifecycle, optional agent framework inside bounded activities or behind an adapter.
