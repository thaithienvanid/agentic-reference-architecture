# Comparison matrices

Scores indicate fit under stated assumptions from 1 (weak) to 5 (strong). They are not objective product rankings. ARA is deliberately penalized for complexity and is unnecessary for many small systems.

## Clean, Hexagonal, and ARA

| Criterion | Clean | Hexagonal | Full ARA |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Inward dependency direction | 5 | 4 | 5 |
| Explicit driving/driven ports | 3 | 5 | 5 |
| Domain isolation | 5 | 4 | 5 |
| Durable agent execution | 1 | 1 | 5 |
| Effect/idempotency/reconciliation model | 1 | 2 | 5 |
| Evaluation and platform governance | 1 | 1 | 5 |
| Initial delivery speed for one bounded LLM call | 5 | 4 | 1 |
| Small-team cognitive load | 4 | 4 | 1–2 |
| Operational infrastructure cost | 5 | 4 | 1–2 |

ARA extends Clean/Hexagonal when durable, tool-using, multi-tenant, or governed execution justifies the added cost. A one-shot summarizer should normally use a much smaller architecture.

## Agent frameworks versus durable engines

| Criterion | Agent framework | Durable workflow engine |
|---|---:|---:|
| Model/tool-loop authoring | 5 | 1 |
| Context/prompt ergonomics | 5 | 1 |
| Durable waits/timers/signals | 3 | 5 |
| Deterministic recovery | 2–3 | 5 |
| Streaming | 5 | 2 |
| Enterprise worker operations | 3 | 5 |
| Learning curve for AI product team | 4 | 2–3 |
| Best role | Bounded agent logic | Lifecycle durability |

Use only the framework when runs are short and low-risk. Use only a durable engine for deterministic processes. Combine them when agentic behavior and durable lifecycle are both material.

## Runtime library versus service

| Criterion | Library only | Service only | Hybrid |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Local development | 5 | 2 | 4 |
| Durable distribution | 1–2 | 5 | 5 |
| Central policy/tenancy | 2 | 5 | 5 |
| Embedded/edge mode | 5 | 1 | 4 |

A library is preferable for one local bounded agent. The hybrid is justified only when local ergonomics and centralized durable governance are both required.

## Adoption falsifiability

The full architecture is not justified unless at least one material requirement exists for durable waits/recovery, significant tool side effects, multi-team/tenant governance, regulated evidence, marketplace execution, or reusable platform operations. If those needs do not exist, prefer a smaller Clean/Hexagonal application with bounded model calls.
