## Source-handling policy

- Product pages support documented public behavior only, not private implementation claims.
- Time-sensitive claims include a retrieval/review date and are revalidated before Stable publication.
- Research findings label **Fact**, **Inference**, and **ARA Recommendation**.
- Comparison matrices identify assumptions, reviewed versions/dates, scoring rubric, and uncertainty.
- Legal and regulatory sources support control mapping, not universal legal conclusions.
- A recommendation belongs to ARA and is not attributed to a vendor unless the source states it.
